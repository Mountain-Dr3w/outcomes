# Interaction Model

> **Primary action:** Choose a case study, understand its evidence, then continue to the next case or contact Drew

The model should make the next meaningful decision obvious while preserving access to depth.

## Selected organizing idea

**Evidence ledger:** Keep the editorial homepage and case-study model, but make evidence level the repeating information spine: measured impact on the homepage, status and proof in each case, decisions paired with artifacts, and an honest end-state

- **Hierarchy:** Measured impact first, selected case studies second; within cases, status and strongest proof before narrative and imagery
- **Interaction bet:** Reviewers scan quantified impact, choose a case, follow decisions with adjacent artifacts, then continue or contact

## Entry and orientation

The homepage opens with Drew's outcomes-first thesis, immediately followed by quantified career impact and then selected current work. Each case card states outcome or capability honestly.

## Primary task path

Scan identity and quantified impact; choose a current case; read status, role, scope, and strongest proof; follow two or three key decisions with adjacent artifacts; review what changed and what remains unproven; continue to the next case or contact Drew.

## Decisions and branches

Reviewers choose between proven career impact and current case studies, then between live builder work, organizational service design, and an experiment still under validation. Labels make evidence level explicit before commitment.

## Visibility and progressive disclosure

Always visible: project status, role, outcome/capability, and strongest proof. In context: narrative decisions and visuals. Deferred: secondary artifacts and implementation detail. Never conceal validation gaps behind visual polish.

## Navigation and context

Homepage anchors remain stable; case pages retain back navigation and add next-case, resume, and email actions at the end. Static routes preserve direct links and browser navigation.

## Feedback and system status

Existing hover, focus, and Reveal behavior remains; links have visible focus, external links remain explicit, and reduced-motion preferences suppress transitions.

## Recovery, interruption, and return

404 route returns home; every case ends with a path to another case or contact; missing public links do not render dead controls; image alt text preserves the evidence description if media fails.
